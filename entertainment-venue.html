Services Info.

We can supply your event with any indoor or outdoor venue that fits your needs. We can also set up a tented outdoor venue for a combined indoor/outdoor experience. Full stage events includes live entertainment with any event booked. Live music events come with rentals of the space with or without the band, or DJ and Audio/Visual setup.

We can provide food, bar, food trucks, casino night, game night, kids events, petting zoos, inflatables, bands of your choice of music, DJ, festival activities, dancers of all cultures, comedians, hosts, weddings, concerts, parties, and all other sorts of performances and events. Our venue also comes with quality sound and sound engineering!



We work with all budgets!
